About Us

WINTIMA was founded in December 2015 by Janet Zeylisa Dauda. Together with a group of young and hardworking change makers, Janet, in 2015, reached out to about 200 school children in the Garu-Tempane district of the Upper East Region, Ghana with learning materials comprising text books, exercise books, story books, pencils and pens. This was to be the start of WINTIMA as she yearned to impart the lives of more rural school children who were deprived of some of these basic school necessities.
